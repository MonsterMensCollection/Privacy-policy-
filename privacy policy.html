Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02/05/2025

At Monster Men's Collection, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app and services, including payment processing through Razorpay.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information (such as name, email address, phone number, billing/shipping address) provided voluntarily.
  • Payment Information (such as UPI ID, credit/debit card details, or other Razorpay-supported methods, securely handled by Razorpay).
  • Device Information (such as IP address, device model, operating system).
  • Usage Data (such as pages visited, time spent in-app).

Payment Gateway (Razorpay)

We use Razorpay as our payment gateway provider. When you make a payment, Razorpay collects and processes your payment information. Razorpay is PCI DSS compliant and uses secure encryption to protect your data.

For more details on how Razorpay handles your data, please visit Razorpay's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process transactions securely via Razorpay.
  • To improve our products and services.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To communicate promotional offers (if opted in).

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Information may be shared with Razorpay and other trusted service providers solely for the purpose of processing payments and operating our services.

Shipment Policy

  • Shipping Charges: We charge a flat rate of $19 for shipping on orders below $99.
  • Free Shipping: Orders equal to or above $99 qualify for free shipping automatically.
  • Processing Time: Orders are crafted and shipped within 2–4 business days after placement.
  • Delivery Time: Delivery usually takes 5–10 business days based on your location.
  • Tracking: Once shipped, you will receive a tracking number via app and email or SMS.
  • Shipping Carriers: We use trusted courier partners for reliable and safe delivery.
  • Incorrect Address: Please ensure your address is correct. We are not responsible for delays or loss due to incorrect details.

Return and Refund Policy

  • Returns: Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are unused, in original condition, and with all tags and packaging intact.
  • Refunds: Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed to your original method of payment within 5–7 business days.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Certain items like innerwear, socks, and custom-tailored pieces are not eligible for return.
  • Return Shipping: Return shipping charges may apply unless the product was damaged or incorrect.
  • For return/refund queries, contact us at Monstermenscollection@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures, including those provided by Razorpay, to protect your information from unauthorized access and misuse.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or Shipment Policy, please contact us at Monstermenscollection@gmail.com.
